Section tags: When you put your content on a web page, it may contains many chapters, headers, footers, or other sections on a web page that is why HTML section tag is used. Section tag defines the section of documents such as chapters, headers, footers or any other sections. The section tag divides the content into section and subsections. The section tag is used when requirements of two headers or footers or any other section of documents needed. Section tag groupes the generic block of related contents. The main advantage of the section tag is, it is a semantic element, which describes its meaning to both browser and developer.
